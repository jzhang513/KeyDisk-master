Introducing KeyDisk:

The World's Thinnest, Most Intelligent Key Holder

Finally, a key holder that is front pocket worthy.

Features:

KeyDisk is a newer, more intelligent way to carry your keys. It is slim, minimal, and elegant.

Specs of KeyDisk Include:

  • Thinnest key-holder to date
  • Offered in multiple metal colors
  • 1.2 ounces
  • 1.8 mm wide
  • Constructed from sandblasted & anodized aluminum
  • Carry up to 6 keys

KeyDisk lets you put up to 6 keys in the thinnest designed keyholder in the world. Comes in multiple colors and can hold anywhere between 1-6 keys.

Other keyholders cannot compare to the thin, sleek look of KeyDisk. Keys on a keyring jingle and take up large amounts of space & normal folding keyholders would require at least 18mm for 6 keys. Not KeyDisk. Standing slim at just 8mm, this is the most sleek a keyholder has ever been.

KeyDisk is so slim that you can barely see it. Now you can slip it in any pocket, even your front pocket, and nobody will be able to tell.

Inception:

After numerous assessments on materials, sizing, and blueprints, we have finished designing the key holder with a sleek construction to ensure a minimal frame incorporating durability for a final prototype.

Who We Are:

Jacob Flores is a graduate student living in San Francisco. KeyDisk Co. was founded by Jacob in early 2013. Being the first Kickstarter for KeyDisk Co., professional help was sought. Collaborating with Steven Ng of Elliot Havok Designs of San Francisco, CA, the KeyDisk project was able to progress from concept to creation. After designing the first KeyDisk with Elliot Havok Designs, the best aluminum manufacturer was selected from various candidates to bring the prototype to life in a professional, high-quality, and timely manner. Jacob will be overseeing this entire process to ensure the project transitions smoothly from start to finish.
